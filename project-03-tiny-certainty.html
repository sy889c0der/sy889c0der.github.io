Tiny Certainty

Unity 3D · Interactive Experience

A Unity-powered 3D interactive experience that invites users to explore a miniature world. Finding certainty in the smallest, most delicate moments of life — each interaction reveals a layer of warmth and wonder.

Process

Built with Unity's Universal Render Pipeline, the project features real-time lighting, custom shader materials, and physics-based interactions. The miniature scenes were meticulously crafted with low-poly aesthetics, creating an intimate diorama-like atmosphere.
